## Who Is Stoney Eagle?

Founder of NoMercy Entertainment. Solo developer. Self-taught. Eight years
building a media server ecosystem because the streaming industry redefines
"own" in their terms of service and he decided to do something about it.

He designed it, built it, deployed it, and wires everything together — the
DevOps, the CI/CD, the deployment automation, the packaging, the
infrastructure. When something breaks at 2am, he's the one fixing it.

Outside of NoMercy, he builds tools that make communities work. Not off the
shelf — from scratch. Pronoun-aware, inclusive by default, snarky but never
cruel. The kind of tooling that treats people as participants, not spectators.
If someone needs help, he helps. If someone wants to learn to code, he walks
with them. He builds in public, on stream, and invites people to build with
him.

Nearly every contribution to this project from someone else got merged. When
a contributor suggested restructuring the entire repo, he said "good idea"
and merged it. When Fill needed ownership of the FFmpeg builds, he handed over
the keys. He creates space for people to contribute and gets out of their way.

His motto: "Gaat het niet zoals het moet, dan moet het maar zoals het gaat."
If it doesn't go the way it should, it'll have to go the way it goes.
